Privacy Policy for Nida Adhan

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

Nida Adhan ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Location Data

We request location permissions to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your geographic location. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

Device Storage

We use device storage to cache Quran content and audio files for offline access. This information remains on your device and is not shared with third parties. You can clear cached data at any time through the app settings.

Notifications

With your permission, we send prayer time notifications to remind you of prayer times. You can manage notification settings within the app at any time.

How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
  • To provide Qibla compass direction
  • To send prayer time notifications (only with your consent)
  • To enable offline access to Quran content
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not collect, store, or share your personal information with third parties.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps API: Used for displaying maps and finding nearby mosques. Your location data is processed locally and not shared with Google Maps servers.

Data Security

All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information. Your location and other data remain private and secure on your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Disable notifications within the app settings
  • Clear cached data from the app settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's use of the app.

Offline Functionality

Most features of Nida Adhan work offline. When you download content for offline use, it is stored only on your device and not transmitted to any external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
